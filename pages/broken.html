Common Questions

We aim to make our service as seamless and "set-and-forget" as possible. Same day, same time. Every week. While each yard has its own personality, a standard maintenance visit generally follows this four-step process:

  • Precision Mowing - We cut your grass to the ideal height for the current season, ensuring a healthy, lush look.
  • String Trimming - We tackle the "hard to reach" spots around fences, trees, and posts that the mower can’t quite get to, as well as curbing the lawn down toward hard edges.
  • Professional Edging - We edge along your driveway and sidewalks, to create that nice gap that makes a lawn pop.
  • Complete Cleanup - We never leave a mess behind. We blow all grass clippings off your hardscapes and back onto the lawn (or haul them away) so your property looks spotless.

For most lawns in Idaho, a weekly schedule is best to maintain the "one-third rule"—never cutting more than 1/3 of the grass blade at once. This keeps the grass healthy and prevents weeds from taking hold.
