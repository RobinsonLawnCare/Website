why choose us ?

Robinson Lawn Care started with a high school student in Rexburg, Idaho, a mower, and a dream. It has since grown into a full scale lawn care company with a team of knowledgeable technicians dedicated to perfecting your lawn.

"Our mission is to handle the heavy lifting outside so you can focus on the life happening inside. We take the yard work off your plate, giving you the freedom to spend your weekends where they count: with family, friends, and the hobbies you love."

We keep this as our top priority with every service we perform. From dethatching your lawn in the spring to fertilizing and weed control by our licensed applicator in the summer months, to leaf cleanup in the fall and everything in between, we are committed to giving you time.
